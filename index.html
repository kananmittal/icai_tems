CALL FOR PAPERS

The 2025 International Conference on Application of Information Technologies in Engineering, Management and Science (ICAI-TEMS) is a newly launched conference under the flagship conference of the IEEE Technology and Engineering Management Society. IEEE ICAI-TEMS will focus mostly on the application of advanced computation in different branches of engineering with its cutting-edge development and practices of technology and innovation management in current business environments.

This event brings together scholars and experts from Europe, Asia and beyond to discuss their work related to advanced computing and their application in different branches of science. It includes an industry forum where industry leaders share insights on the trend, impacts, opportunities, and challenges in enhancing global business competitiveness. This conference provides studies on the concepts, theories, and best practices in the field of technology management.

General Objectives

  • Promote a collaborative environment between the academic community in search of improvement and attractiveness of innovation, research, and investments.
  • Promote dialogue between the scientific community and the main national and international Higher Education Institutions.
  • Enable the digital transformation.
  • Promote the integrated advancement of the different areas of science with a view to improving the quality of life of citizens and of society in general.
  • Encourage young science talents to publish their work in the conference topics.
  • Present and debate results from different science and innovation groups in Information Technologies and Systems, allowing the emergence of new doctoral candidates.

Accepted papers will be submitted for possible inclusion into IEEE Xplore
